Biography

Im a Prime Minister's Research Fellow (PMRF) in the Department of Chemistry with Prof. S. Velmathi at the National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli (NITT). I Obtained an MSc from with Central University of Tamil Nadu in 2019, and a BSc from Victoria College, University of Calicut in 2017, all in Chemistry. My Broad Area of Research Is Organic Sensors for Real Time Application. I'm Trying to Develop Supramolecular Probes for The Selective and Rapid Detection of Chemical Warfare Agents with An Aim to Apply for Real Life Samples. I Aim to Develop a Device Capable of Onsite Detection of Even Trace Amounts of These Noxious Nerve Agents with Potentially High Selectivity...

Previously I have done an internship as part of INSA Fellowship in Department of Chemistry, Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM) with Dr. P. Anbarasan. Futher I have qulaified GATE and CSIR-JRF in Chemical Sciences
